Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events. For smaller gatherings, 4-8 weeks is typically sufficient.

Do you offer customized packages?

Absolutely! We understand every event is unique. We're happy to create customized packages tailored to your specific needs and budget.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Islamabad and Rawalpindi, but we're available for destination events throughout Pakistan.

Can I visit your office for consultation?

Yes! We encourage in-person consultations. Please schedule an appointment by calling us or using the booking form.
